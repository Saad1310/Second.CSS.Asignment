BREAKING NEWS : GOLD PRICES IN PAKISTAN

All-Pakistan Gems and Jewellers Sarafa Association (APGJSA), the price of 24-karat gold increased by Rs5,300, reaching Rs424,162 per tola. Similarly, 10 grams of 24-karat gold saw an uptick of Rs4,544, settling at Rs363,650.


Throughout October, gold prices in Pakistan have climbed significantly, with an overall rise of Rs17,384 per tola from the starting price of Rs406,778 earlier this month.
